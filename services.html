Service 01

Total Station & Land Survey Dubai

Our total station surveys cover all aspects of land measurement in Dubai — from initial topographic surveys through to boundary demarcation, construction setting-out and as-built documentation. All deliverables are produced in WGS84 coordinates, UTM Zone 40N and EGM2008 height datum as required by Dubai Municipality.

We use Leica TS16 and Trimble S7 total stations providing ±2mm positional accuracy. Every control point is independently verified before fieldwork begins.
