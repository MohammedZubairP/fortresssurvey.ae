Common Questions

Quick Answers

How fast can you be on site?

Within 24 hours of quote confirmation for most Dubai locations — same-day mobilisation is often possible for urgent requests.

Are your surveys accepted by Dubai Municipality?

Yes — all deliverables are produced in WGS84 / UTM Zone 40N / EGM2008 to DM standards, and we handle DM e-submissions directly where required.

What do you need from me to give a quote?

A plot number or site location, the survey type you need, and any drawings you have. That's enough for a fixed price within 2 hours.

Do you work outside Dubai?

We operate exclusively within the Emirate of Dubai — this focus is what lets us mobilise fast and know every authority requirement inside out.
