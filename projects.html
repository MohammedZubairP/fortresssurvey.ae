The Work We're Built For

Every project type below reflects work our engineers have delivered across Dubai — and stand ready to deliver for you under the Fortress name.

🏠 Villas & Residential Plots

Most Requested

The bread and butter of Dubai surveying — supporting owners, contractors and consultants through the full villa lifecycle from plot purchase to completion certificate.

Plot boundary verification against DLD title coordinates
Topographic surveys for villa design and landscaping
Setting out of foundations, columns and boundary walls
Extension and modification surveys with DM documentation
As-built surveys for Building Completion Certificates

🏢 Towers & High-Rise

Precision Critical

Vertical construction magnifies small errors — high-rise work demands rigorous control transfer, verticality monitoring and floor-by-floor discipline.

Primary control networks and vertical control transfer
Column and core wall verticality checks per floor
Floor level surveys and slab deflection checks
Facade and cladding alignment surveys
Unit area measurement supporting RERA compliance

🏭 Industrial & Logistics

Jebel Ali • Dubai South • Al Quoz

Warehouses and factories where floor precision and racking geometry directly affect operations — a specialised discipline our team knows well.

Floor flatness surveys against racking specifications
Racking anchor setting out for installation crews
Mezzanine as-built surveys for DM approval
Crane rail alignment surveys
Facility expansion boundary and topographic surveys

🛣️ Roads & Infrastructure

RTA Standards

Corridor work with long control networks, live-traffic method planning and drainage tolerances where millimetres matter.

Corridor topographic surveys for design
Alignment and kerb line setting out
Drainage invert setting out and verification
Layer-by-layer pavement level checks
As-built documentation in RTA formats

⚓ Marine & Waterfront

Hydrographic

Dubai's coastline keeps growing — and marine works need seabed data, dredge volumes and structures surveyed above and below the waterline.

Bathymetric surveys of marinas and channels
Dredging volume baseline and verification surveys
Quay wall and marine structure as-builts
Beach profile monitoring surveys

☀️ Solar & Energy

Growing Sector

From Shams Dubai rooftops to ground-mounted arrays — survey data that drives yield modelling, pile positioning and structure verification.

Terrain modelling for array layout and grading design
Drone mapping of large sites with ground control
Pile position setting out at production pace
Rooftop measured surveys for solar design
As-built verification of mounting structures
