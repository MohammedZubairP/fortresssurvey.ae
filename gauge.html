How It Works

From Sensor to Dashboard in 4 Steps

STEP 01

Sensor Installation

Our engineers install settlement markers, tilt meters or crack gauges at agreed monitoring points across your site.

STEP 02

Baseline Reading

An initial zero-reading is taken and logged as the baseline for all future movement comparisons.

STEP 03

Live Monitoring Begins

Sensors transmit data into the Gauge platform automatically — your dashboard goes live immediately.

STEP 04

Alerts & Reporting

Set your thresholds once. Gauge handles the rest — alerts, graphs and reports run themselves.
