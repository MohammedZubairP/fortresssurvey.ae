How As-Built Surveys Prevent
Handover Disputes in Dubai

June 2026 • 5 min read • By Fortress Surveying Engineering Services

Project handover is where unresolved discrepancies between design and construction surface most painfully — and most expensively. An accurate, independently certified as-built survey is one of the most effective tools developers and contractors have to prevent disputes from escalating at this critical stage.

Where Handover Disputes Typically Originate

  • Area discrepancies — constructed unit areas differing from sold or contracted areas
  • Level variations — finished floor levels not matching design, affecting drainage falls or ceiling heights
  • Boundary encroachments — structures extending beyond approved building lines
  • Unrecorded variations — field changes made during construction without proper documentation

How Independent As-Built Survey Helps

An as-built survey conducted by an independent, DM-registered surveyor provides objective, defensible measurement data that isn't influenced by either party's commercial interest in the outcome. This neutral documentation becomes the reference point when disputes arise — significantly reducing the time and cost of resolving disagreements compared to relying on conflicting internal records.

Best Practice Timing

StageAs-Built Survey Purpose
Practical completionEstablish baseline as-built record before snagging
Pre-handoverConfirm final areas and levels match contracted specifications
Post-snaggingVerify any remedial works completed correctly

Documentation That Holds Up

Surveys intended to support potential dispute resolution should be thorough, dated, and produced by a surveyor with no commercial stake in the outcome. Photographic records alongside measured data add further weight if a dispute does proceed to formal resolution.
