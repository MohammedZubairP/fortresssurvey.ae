Detailed Drone Survey Scope

Every sub-service below is delivered in-house by our licensed engineers — no subcontracting, one accountable team.

Orthomosaic Mapping

High-resolution georeferenced aerial maps at 2–3cm GSD for design, records and marketing.

Photogrammetric Terrain Models

Dense point clouds and DTMs from imagery, controlled by ground survey points.

Stockpile Volume Surveys

Fast, repeatable stockpile measurement — far more accurate than truck counts.

Construction Progress Mapping

Regular flights documenting site progress with comparable, measurable datasets.

Corridor & Route Mapping

Long linear sites mapped in a fraction of ground-survey time.

Inspection Imagery

Roof, facade and structure imagery for condition review without access equipment.

Cut & Fill Analysis

Surface-to-surface comparison between flights quantifying earthworks movement.

DCAA Permitting Handled

All flight permissions and no-fly coordination managed by us.
